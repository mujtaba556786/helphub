6The Booking Process

The booking flow works as follows:

  • Request: A Customer sends a booking request specifying the service, date, time, and a message to the Helper.
  • Confirmation: The Helper accepts or declines the request. A contract is formed between Customer and Helper at the point of acceptance.
  • Service: The service takes place at the agreed time and location.
  • Completion: The Customer marks the booking as completed.
  • Review: Both parties may leave a rating and review. Reviews are moderated before publication.

HelpHub is not a party to the service contract formed between Customer and Helper. Any disputes arising from a booking must be resolved directly between the parties concerned.
