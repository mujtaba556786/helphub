3How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data solely to:

  • Operate and improve the HelpMate marketplace.
  • Match Customers with suitable Helpers based on location and service categories.
  • Send booking notifications and transactional emails (e.g. magic sign-in links).
  • Display your profile to other users when relevant to a booking or task.
  • Moderate content and enforce our Terms & Conditions.
  • Comply with legal obligations under German and EU law.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making that produces legal effects.

4Who Can See Your Data

Other HelpMate users can see the following parts of your profile:

  • Your name, profile photo, bio, service categories, hourly rate, and city.
  • Your average rating and published reviews.
  • Your availability status.

When a booking is created, the Customer and Helper involved can see each other's name, photo, and contact details for the purpose of arranging the service.

HelpMate administrators can access account data, messages, and booking records for moderation and support purposes only.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

5Location Data

If you grant location permission in your browser, HelpMate reads your GPS coordinates to show Helpers near you on the map. This real-time GPS position is not stored on our servers — it is used only in the current session.

Your address (street, city, country) is stored as part of your profile if you enter it manually. You can update or delete your address at any time via the Edit Profile page.

You may revoke GPS permission at any time through your browser or device settings. The app continues to work without GPS — Helpers will be shown based on your saved address instead.

6Authentication Tokens & Local Storage

HelpMate uses a passwordless magic-link authentication system. When you sign in:

  • A short-lived link is emailed to you (expires in 15 minutes, single use).
  • On clicking the link, a JWT access token and a refresh token are issued.
  • These tokens are stored in your browser's localStorage — never as cookies sent to third parties.
  • The access token expires after a short period; the refresh token allows silent renewal without asking you to sign in again.

Your language preference is also stored in localStorage under the key helpmate_lang.

No tracking cookies or third-party analytics scripts are loaded by HelpMate.

7Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: Personal data is deleted within 90 days of your account deletion request.
  • Booking records: Anonymised booking records (without personal identifiers) may be retained for up to 3 years for business analytics.
  • Legal records: Terms acceptance records are retained for 6 years as required under German commercial law (§ 257 HGB).
  • Auth tokens: Expired tokens are purged automatically from our database.

8Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
  • JWT tokens signed with a secure secret key.
  • No plaintext passwords — authentication is entirely passwordless.
  • Magic links are single-use and expire after 15 minutes.
  • Database access is restricted to the application backend only.

In the event of a personal data breach that poses a risk to your rights, we will notify the relevant supervisory authority (BfDI or applicable Landesbeauftragter) within 72 hours and affected users without undue delay, as required by GDPR Article 33 and 34.
